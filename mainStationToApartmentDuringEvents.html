Main Station to Dave's Apartment During Events

Occasionally, when there are major events in the stadium, Tram 2 does not run. When this happens, the best route back to the apartment is to take a train to Hardbrücke and then bus 33 to Hubertus. From there, it’s a 3-4 minute walk to the apartment.

The route is:

  • Zurich HB → Hardbrücke (by train, 1 stop)
  • Hardbrücke → Hubertus (by bus 33, 4 stops)

Details for each step:

Zurich HB → Hardbrücke: Multiple trains are available on this route, generally leaving from platform 41/42, downstairs in the train station. The possible trains you can take include the S5, S6, S7, S9, S11, S12, S15, and S16.

Hardbrücke → Hubertus: When you get off the train at Hardbrücke, there are 2 elevators on the platform. Get on the one that shows 33 Triemli and 72 Morgental, and go up to the top level.

From the street, take bus 33 for 4 stops, to Hubertus.

Hubertus to the apartment: From Hubertus, just walk up Letzigraben, towards the stadium.
