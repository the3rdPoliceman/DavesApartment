Triemli to Uetliberg/Felsenegg

Time: 1.5 hours to Uto Kulm, a further 1 hour to Felsenegg

A nice little hike up through the woods to the Uto Kulm Hotel, which provides a great view of the city and lake. It climbs around 500m (1500ft) altitude, and the walking surface is good.

From the Uto Kulm you can hike further along the ridge to Felsenegg, or return home from the Uetliberg station just below the hotel

If you go on to Felsenegg you can return by taking the cable car down and then the train back to Zürich from Adliswil. The cable car runs until the early evening, but best to check running times by searching the SBB website for Felsenegg to Adliswil (Luftseilbahnstation)
