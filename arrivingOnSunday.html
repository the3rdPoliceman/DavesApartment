Arriving on Sunday

If you're arriving on a Sunday, it's useful to know that almost all shops and supermarkets in Zurich will be closed. However, there are a few exceptions.

Open Supermarkets on Sunday

Migros at Zurich Airport

Located inside Zurich Airport in the shopping area.
After you come through security, if you follow the signs for trains etc. you'll cross to another building. You're looking for the middle floor (L0), which has most of the shops.
The Migros supermarket is at one end, and has a large orange sign.
Find it on Google Maps

Migros at Zurich Main Train Station (Hauptbahnhof)

The main station has a lot of shops one level below ground, and there you'll find a Migros supermarket.
If you follow signs for platform 41 and 42, the supermarket is near where the escalators go down to those platforms.
Find it on Google Maps

Coop at Stadelhofen

Located underground, directly under the tracks at Stadelhofen train station, close to Bellevue tram stop.
Find it on Google Maps

Migros at Enge

Located at ground level within Enge train station.
Find it on Google Maps
