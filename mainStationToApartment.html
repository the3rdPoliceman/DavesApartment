Main Station to Dave's Apartment by Tram

Coming to the apartment using public transport involves taking 2 trams. The trams run frequently, so you don’t have to worry about missing connections.

The route is:

  • Zürich, Bahnhofplatz/HB → Stauffacher (by tram 3 or 14)
  • Stauffacher → Letzigrund (by tram 2)

Details for each step:

Zürich, Bahnhofplatz/HB → Stauffacher (by tram 3 or 14):

Find your way to the tram stop Zürich, Bahnhofplatz/HB. The easiest way to get there is by taking the south exit from the main hall of the train station. This exit will be to the right if you are looking towards the closed end of the hall. (Google Maps is also handy to locate the tram stop.)

From this stop, take tram 14 in the direction of Triemli, or tram 3 in the direction of Albisrieden, and get off at Stauffacher (3 stops).

Stauffacher → Letzigrund (by tram 2):

Take tram 2 in the direction of Schlieren Geissweid to tram stop Letzigrund (6 stops). The Letzigrund tram stop is just across the road from the apartment.

From the tram stop, you can already see my apartment building.
