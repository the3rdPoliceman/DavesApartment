Airport to Dave's Apartment

By Public Transport

Coming to the apartment using public transport involves a couple of changes, but the buses and trams run frequently, so you don’t have to worry about missing connections.

The best route is:

  • Zurich Airport → Hardbrücke (by train S16, 2 stops)
  • Hardbrücke → Albisriederplatz (by bus 72, 2 stops)
  • Albisriederplatz → Letzigrund (by tram 2, 1 stop)
Details for each step:

Zurich Airport → Hardbrücke: Take the S16 train from the airport, which leaves at 1 and 31 minutes past every hour (e.g., 6:31, 7:01, 7:31, etc.). It normally leaves from platform 3, and as the airport is the last stop, it’s impossible to take it in the wrong direction.

Hardbrücke → Albisriederplatz: When you get off the train at Hardbrücke, there are 2 elevators facing each other on the platform. Get on the one that shows 33 Triemli and 72 Morgental, and go up to the street.

From the street, take bus 72 Morgental for 2 stops, to Albisriederplatz. Once you get off the bus at Albisriederplatz, make your way to the middle of the roundabout to take the tram.

Albisriederplatz → Letzigrund: Take tram 2 in the direction of Schlieren Geissweid for 1 stop. The Letzigrund tram stop is just across the road from the apartment.

Taxi or Uber

If you prefer to take a taxi or Uber, I would recommend Uber. The cost for an Uber is normally around 40 francs (this can vary depending on the day and time), but a taxi will generally cost twice as much.
